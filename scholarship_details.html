GenTex Scholarship 2024

Location: USA

Overview

The Bestway Foundation Scholarship is a prestigious scholarship program aimed at supporting students from Pakistan to pursue postgraduate studies in the United Kingdom. Established by the Bestway Group, one of the largest multinational conglomerates, the scholarship is designed to promote education and professional development. The foundation partners with leading UK universities to provide students with opportunities to study in a range of disciplines, particularly in fields that are crucial for the development of Pakistan.

Countries Intakes Details

    The Bestway Foundation Scholarship is specifically targeted at Pakistani students. The application periods and intakes vary slightly depending on the partner universities:

  • Application Period: Typically opens in autumn and closes in early spring.
  • Participating Universities: Includes prestigious institutions such as the University of Cambridge, University of Oxford, Imperial College London, and University of Kent.

Who Can Apply?

  • Be a citizen of Pakistan and permanently reside there.
  • Hold a first-class undergraduate degree from a recognized university.
  • Meet the English language requirements of the chosen university.
  • Apply to study an eligible full-time postgraduate course at one of the participating universities.
  • Demonstrate a commitment to contributing to the development of Pakistan.

What Types of Awards are Available?

    The Bestway Foundation Scholarship supports full-time postgraduate study in various disciplines. The types of awards available include:

  • Master’s Degrees: One-year or two-year master's programs in fields such as Engineering, Computer Science, and Business Administration.
  • Doctoral Degrees: PhD programs focusing on research that can benefit Pakistan’s development

What Financial Support is Available?

    The Bestway Foundation Scholarship provides comprehensive financial support, which includes:

  • Full Tuition Fees: Covers all tuition fees required by the university.
  • Stipend: A monthly allowance for living expenses, which is set according to the host university's cost of living estimates.

Hear from Alumni

   Alumni of the Bestway Foundation Scholarship have achieved significant success in their academic and professional careers. For example, Dr. Ayesha Khan, an alumna, completed her Master’s in Public Health at the University of Cambridge and has since played a pivotal role in improving healthcare systems in rural Pakistan. Another alumnus, Mr. Farooq Ahmed, pursued a PhD in Electrical Engineering at Imperial College London and has contributed to groundbreaking research in renewable energy technologies. Testimonials from Bestway Scholars often highlight how the scholarship has enabled them to gain invaluable skills and knowledge, which have significantly advanced their careers and their capacity to contribute to Pakistan's development.

Other Details

    The Bestway Foundation Scholarship not only provides financial assistance but also fosters a supportive network among scholars and alumni. Recipients are encouraged to actively participate in the academic and cultural life of their host universities. Scholars engage in various events, seminars, and workshops that promote leadership development and cross-cultural understanding. The foundation emphasizes the importance of returning to Pakistan after completing their studies to apply their new skills and knowledge to contribute to the country's progress. For more detailed information, prospective applicants are encouraged to visit the official websites of the Bestway Foundation and the participating universities. They should review the specific application requirements and deadlines for their chosen courses of study.
