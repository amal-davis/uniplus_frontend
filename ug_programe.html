Undergraduate Programme
Paving the Path to Top UK Universities:
UNIPLUS Global Education offers a range of UK study options tailored to your academic background and goals. Whether you're aiming for a prestigious university or a specialized medical school, we have the program for you.
  • International Foundation Program: This intensive one-year program bridges the gap between high school and undergraduate studies in the UK. Focused on developing your specialist knowledge, language skills, and study habits, it prepares you for academic success at a UK university.
  • International Year One: Equipping you with the skills and knowledge equivalent to the first year of a UK undergraduate degree, this program allows you to enter directly into the second year. This pathway saves you time while ensuring you graduate alongside your domestic peers in subjects like architecture, business, psychology, and many more.
  • International Diploma (Scotland): Offered at our Scottish centers, this program is equivalent to the second year of a four-year Scottish degree. Upon successful completion, you gain advanced entry into the third year of an undergraduate program.
