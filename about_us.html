About Us

Our Story

UNIPLUS Global Education's story begins with Greendome Global Education and Training, a company founded in London, UK. Greendome boasts a distinguished reputation for success in the education sector, built upon the 20-year experience of its founder, Mr. Abdullah Mohammed Aljumaah. Mr. Aljumaah's extensive background includes roles as a counsellor at the Saudi Cultural Bureau in London, director of the office of the Deputy Minister of Higher Education, cultural attach√© in Oman, and supervising academics in Dubai. His prior experience also encompasses various positions in the Ministry of Information and the office of the Minister of Higher Education.

Recognizing the potential to empower students worldwide, UNIPLUS Global Education was launched in India. This expansion aimed to bring Greendome's excellence and proven approach to students seeking top-notch international education opportunities.

A Commitment to Affordability and Accessibility

UNIPLUS Global Education stands out by offering affordable pathways for students to pursue their academic dreams at prestigious universities around the globe. This commitment to affordability makes quality international education more accessible to a wider range of students.

Branching Out: Reaching the Middle East

UNIPLUS Global Education's commitment to serving students internationally is further emphasized by the establishment of a branch in Saudi Arabia. This expansion extends their reach to the Middle East, providing students in the region with the same valuable resources and support offered in India and the UK.

The Journey Continues

This company overview establishes UNIPLUS Global Education's dedication to expanding access to high-quality international education for students across diverse regions. By combining Greendome's proven track record with a commitment to affordability, UNIPLUS Global Education positions itself as a leader in the field of study abroad services.

Empowering Students

UNIPLUS Global Education empowers students by offering a comprehensive suite of services, including:

Application guidance and support: Assisting students throughout the application process, from selecting universities to crafting compelling essays.

University selection: Helping students identify programs that align with their academic goals and interests.

Accommodation arrangements: Providing guidance on finding suitable housing options near their chosen university.

Social, legal, and academic guidance: Offering support and resources to help students navigate the cultural, legal, and academic aspects of studying abroad.
